In the Mr. Robot series is starring Elliot, a security engineer by day, hacker by night, who works at a huge technology company called E corp. He lives alone in his apartment, and it seems his only friend is his childhood neighbor Angela, with whom he is secretly in love with. His life takes a turn when he meets group of hackers who are willing to recruit him in their mission to expose the people responsible for the corruption of the modern world. The rest of this series is going to circulate around this plan, and his moral indecisiveness about whether it should be up to him or not to change the world.
